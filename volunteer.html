Volunteer With Us

Every day, families across Charlotte face food insecurity due to recent SNAP benefit cuts. Back2Benefits is here to bring hope, meals, and community support. Join us and help make a difference — one meal at a time.

How You Can Help

Every food box packed, every meal served, and every hand extended creates hope for families who have lost vital food support. Together, we can fight hunger one neighborhood at a time.


Volunteer Positions

  • Food Drive Assistant: Collect and organize donations.
  • Community Kitchen Helper: Cook and serve meals.
  • Delivery Volunteer: Deliver food boxes to homes.
  • Event Organizer: Assist with logistics at food events.
  • Outreach Advocate: Spread awareness and connect neighbors.
