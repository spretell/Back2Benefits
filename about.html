About Back2Benefits

Back2Benefits is a nonprofit organization focused on supporting individuals and families impacted by the recent suspension of SNAP benefits. We connect communities by providing local food resources, any information on SNAP updates, meal assistance forms, and volunteering events so no one has to face hunger alone.

Why Our Work Matters

47 million

people in the U.S. face hunger, including children, seniors, and working families.

1 in 5

children live in food-insecure households.

$187 per month

is the average loss in grocery support when SNAP assistance is interrupted.

*Statistics provided by Feeding America and NPR

Our Mission

Our mission at Back2Benefits is to provide individuals access to nutritious food, resources, and a supportive community.

Our Vision

We envision a future where no one has to choose between groceries, rent, and other essentials. This future would be strengthened through the power of community.
