Back2Benefits

Our mission is to support individuals and communities who have recently been affected by the SNAP benefits suspension. Join us to help families through our resources and support.

Why are SNAP benefits important?

SNAP benefits help millions of people access food when their income is not enough. When benefits are delayed, reduced, or suspended, families are forced into difficult situations.

Hunger in America is a crisis.

“47 million people face hunger in the U.S.—including 13 million children. Hunger touches every U.S. community, including yours.” - Feeding America

With SNAP benefits being suspended, millions across the country are fighting hunger and hardship. Together, we can help.

About Us

Back2Benefits is a nonprofit organization that helps and connects people with local food pantries, resources, and volunteering events against this food crisis.
